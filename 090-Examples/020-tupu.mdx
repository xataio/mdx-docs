Tupu is a non-profit initiative that offers free mentorship to individuals who identify as women, people of color, and members of the LGBT community who work in the tech industry.

The application was initially built on Airtable, but later ported to Xata.

- [Explore the app](https://tupu.io)
- [View the Source Code](https://github.com/tupuio/tupuapp)

### Features

This cloud-based relational database and collaboration platform allows users to organize and store information in a spreadsheet-like format, and highlights the following key features of Xata:

- **[Data aggregation](/docs/sdk/aggregate):** Consolidate data from various sources or groups to get a comprehensive view and make insightful analyses.
- **[Form handling](/docs/sdk/get):** Manage user input with validation.
- **[Search functionality](/docs/sdk/search):** Locate specific data points in large datasets.
